Analytics Tools for User Feedback

1. Hotjar - Heatmaps, session recordings, surveys, and feedback widgets.

2. UserTesting - Remote usability testing with real users.

3. Usabilla (by SurveyMonkey) - Collect feedback via embedded buttons, forms, and targeted surveys.

4. Qualtrics - Advanced survey and experience management platform.

5. Usersnap - Visual feedback tool for bug reporting and UX comments.

6. Typeform - Conversational forms and surveys.

7. Pendo - Product analytics + in-app feedback.

8. SurveyMonkey - Flexible survey creation and analysis.

9. Google Forms + Google Analytics - Basic feedback collection + behavior tracking.

10. Canny - Feature request and feedback board.
